# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-30

## Summary
ChatGPT Text Highlight stores your saved snippets locally in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your data.

## What the extension does
The extension lets you select text inside ChatGPT conversations and save it as snippets for later review.

## Data we collect
The extension does not collect data on a server. It stores selected snippet content locally in your browser, along with metadata needed to navigate back to the source (conversation ID, optional project ID, timestamp, and the page URL where the snippet was created).

## Data storage
All snippets and related metadata are stored locally using `chrome.storage.local` and never leave your device.

## Data sharing
We do not share any data with third parties.
